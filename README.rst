Models
------

- **fountain_pump_nozzle**: Nozzle adapter for fountain pumps
- **kaleo_pusher**: Pusher mechanism for Kaleo devices

-----
Usage
-----

1. Download the STL files from the respective directories
2. Slice with your preferred slicer software
3. Print with appropriate settings for your printer and material
